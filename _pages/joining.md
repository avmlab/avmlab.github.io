We’re currently looking for motivated members to join our group. If you’re passionate about computational neuroscience and machine learning, we’d love to hear from you!

**Bachelor / Master thesis:** There are several possibilities for a Bachelor or Master thesis in the lab, primarily aimed at Physics students at RWTH Aachen, but students from related programs are welcome to reach out. Background experience in all relevant topics is not expected — curiosity and motivation matter most.

**PhD / Postdoc:** Currently, there is no funded position available in the lab. Nonetheless, please reach out with a brief introduction and your CV if our research resonates with you to discuss possible future opportunities or joint applications for funding.

**Get in touch:** Drop us an [email](mailto:alexander.van.meegen@rwth-aachen.de) or stop by Office 112 in Modulbau Physik.
